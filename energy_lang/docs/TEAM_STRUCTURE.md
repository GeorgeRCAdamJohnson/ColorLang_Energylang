# EnergyLang Team Structure & Roles

## Core Teams
- **Compiler/Language Team:** Language design, parser, AST/IR, backend, optimization
- **Runtime/Systems Team:** Adaptive runtime, hardware detection, scheduling, legacy support
- **Profiling/Optimization Team:** Energy telemetry, visualization, auto-tuning, ML-guided refactoring
- **IDE/Tooling Team:** IDE plugins, CLI tools, build system/CI/CD integration
- **Docs/Community Team:** Documentation, guides, community portal, sample projects
- **Product/PM/QA:** Roadmap, requirements, testing, release management

## Estimated Staffing (AI/Industry Standard)
- 10–20+ engineers (across teams)
- 2–3 designers (UI/UX, visualization)
- 2–3 technical writers
- 2–3 product managers
- QA, DevOps, community managers

## Collaboration
- Agile, parallel workstreams
- AI-assisted code generation and review
- Regular cross-team syncs and milestone reviews

---

*Team structure is designed for scalable, real-world delivery and market impact.*
